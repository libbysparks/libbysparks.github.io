Bud's Buffet is a family owned and operated Restaurant established in 1988. It is located in the heart of Downtown Sacramento on 10th street, between J and K streets. It's a short walk from the State Capitol Building and the Cathedral of the Blessed Sacrament, few blocks away from the Golden 1 Center, and surrounded by all major hotels, theaters, and local attractions.
